Welcome to the PHTC Training Environment

The New York City-Long Island-Lower Tri County Public Health Training Center is pleased to offer a comprehensive collection of competency-based public health and public health-related trainings. Our training programs are free, high-quality, and interactive – designed to fully engage learners and impart needed knowledge and skills for the public health workforce.

In order to enroll in these programs, you will need to complete a one-time registration form. In registering, you will create your own personal dashboard where you will gain access to all of our training resources. In addition, from your dashboard you can:

  • Keep track of progress through and completion of different trainings
  • Print and store certificates of completion
  • Request continuing education credits
  • Update your user profile

New trainings are always being developed and will be added regularly to the training catalog. Be sure to log-in regularly to check for updates!
